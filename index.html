Purpose of the Test:

This tool is a prototype inspired by the Test of Variables of Attention (TOVA), a type of Continuous Performance Test (CPT). It was created as part of a graduation project to test design ideas and user experience. It is for demonstration and research purposes only ‚Äî it is not a medical diagnostic tool.

What You Will Do:

You will complete a short series of repetitive tasks that measure reaction time and attention. It should take about 30 minutes.

Voluntary Participation:

Your participation is completely voluntary. You may stop at any time, for any reason, without any penalty.

Risks & Discomfort:

There are no known significant risks, but the test may feel repetitive or tiring. Take breaks if needed, or exit at any time.

Data Privacy:

This is a demo version. No data will be collected or stored by the system. When you finish, your results will be available for you to download locally. You are responsible for saving or sharing them if you wish ‚Äî the data is not sent anywhere else.

How Your Data Is Used:

Since no data is stored on the server, we do not keep any of your test results. If you choose to share your results with others (for example, with a healthcare professional), that is entirely up to you.
